Survival Trail

This trail is a collection of events scattered through Bitcamp that put your skills to the test. From programming and design, to jenga blocks and water pong, this trail is built to test your resourcefulness across a wide range of subjects.

Terrapin Trail

This Trail is a guided intro to hacking for beginners. Join hands-on workshops that guide your through the process of starting a project, using APIs, and deploying your idea.
