Impact Trail

This trail has to do with with making an impact on the world. Students get the chance to work directly with nonprofits and NGOs to produce solutions to real world problems. For the duration of the event, hackers will receive access to one­on­one mentorship from professionals from our partner organizations and Project Delta team.

  1. Ice (Cream) Breaker

    • Meet and greet your fellow social hackers at the Ice Cream Social! We’ll all head over together.
    • Friday @ 10 PM

  2. Kickoff Event

    • We’ll introduce this Trail. Nonprofits and NGOs who are partnering with us will talk about who they are and the problems they are currently facing around the world.
    • Friday @ 11PM

  3. Social Change Whiteboard Brainstorm Workshop

    • We’ll bring the whiteboards, you bring the ideas.
    • Friday @ 11 PM

  4. First NGO Checkpoint

    • Students pitch ideas and prototypes to NGOs, and NGOs give feedback in order to finetune hacks to their needs.
    • Saturday @ 11 AM

  5. Fun Event

    • This may be a Ping Pong tournament, or it may be a scavenger hunt. Whatever it is, it’ll be a fun event. You’ll find out on Saturday!
    • Saturday @ 4 PM

  6. Second NGO Checkpoint

    • Check back in to see your progress and start planning for the expo.
    • Saturday @ 11 PM
