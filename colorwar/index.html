Calling all creatives!

Welcome to Bitcamp's second annual COLORWAR design competition! COLORWAR is a chance for our Bitcamp participants and community to get their creative juices flowing in intense design challenges.

Submit Now

How It Work

Part 1: Submissions

Create a visual that expresses your interpretation of "Imagine Tomorrow," this year's Bitcamp theme. Show us that image on Twitter with the hashtag #COLORWAR. In order to be selected for the next round, you must be registered and planning to attend Bitcamp, but everyone can submit for fun (and receive a COLORWAR sticker and shirt).

Part 2: The Main Event

We'll select five winners out of all the submissions for the main event at Bitcamp. Contestants will battle it out through five rounds of knockout design challenges. Judges and the crowd will determine one victor.

Prizes

Everyone who submits will receive limited-edition COLORWAR shirts and stickers. The Top 5 contestants will compete for HD monitors, Wacom tablets, Pencils by FiftyThree, Dribbble invites, and more!
